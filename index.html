CARNEGIE MELLON UNIVERSITY + INTERNATIONAL JUSTICE MISSION

SEEK JUSTICE

We are Carnegie Mellon University's chapter of International Justice Mission, who seek to make the oppressed heard. Join us to learn about human trafficking, pray for the world, and support IJM with fundraisers.

GET INVOLVED

WHAT WE DO

RAISE AWARENESS

We bring awareness to ourselves and our peers about issues of social justice and human rights and the work IJM is doing.

RAISE FUNDS

We raise funds for IJM to support the rescue and rehabilitation of victims of human trafficking across the globe.

RAISE VOICES

We call upon our organization and our peers to raise our voices for those who cannot be heard.
